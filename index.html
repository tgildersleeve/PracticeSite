Beaches
Moonlight
Known by locals as Encinitas's "mainstream" beach. Usually the most packed out of the bunch. Offers a snack shack, volleyball courts, fire pits, and a playground.
Swami's
Swami's has a nice surf break, but a small parking lot. Make sure to clamber around the rock reef or stop nextdoor by the Self Realization Gardens to meditate.
Grandview
Located snugly between Beacons and Ponto, Grandview is a swell beach with a fantastic view from its staircase. Its southern cliffs are also dotted with many a private staircase.
